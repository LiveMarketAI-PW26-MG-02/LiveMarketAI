    <div class="container">
        <h1>Capstone Project Code of Conduct</h1>
        
        <div class="purpose">
            <h2>Purpose</h2>
            <p>This Code of Conduct establishes the expectations and responsibilities for all team members participating in the capstone project. It ensures equitable contribution, accountability, and successful project completion.</p>
        </div>
        
        <div class="core-principle">
            <h2>Core Principle: Universal Contribution Requirement</h2>
            <p>Every team member must contribute to the capstone project, regardless of circumstances.</p>
            <p style="margin-top: 15px; font-size: 0.95em; font-weight: normal;">This requirement is fundamental and non-negotiable. All participants accepted into the capstone project commit to active participation and meaningful contribution throughout the project lifecycle.</p>
        </div>
        
        <h2>Contribution Expectations</h2>
        
        <div class="section-box">
            <h3>1. Active Participation</h3>
            <p>All team members are required to:</p>
            <ul>
                <li>Attend all scheduled team meetings and check-ins</li>
                <li>Respond to team communications within 24 hours</li>
                <li>Participate in project planning and decision-making processes</li>
                <li>Complete assigned tasks by agreed-upon deadlines</li>
            </ul>
        </div>
        
        <div class="section-box">
            <h3>2. Quality of Work</h3>
            <p>Each team member must:</p>
            <ul>
                <li>Deliver work that meets established quality standards</li>
                <li>Seek clarification when requirements are unclear</li>
                <li>Request support or resources when facing challenges</li>
                <li>Accept and implement constructive feedback</li>
            </ul>
        </div>
        
        <div class="section-box">
            <h3>3. Equitable Distribution</h3>
            <ul>
                <li>Work will be distributed fairly among all team members</li>
                <li>Each member's contributions will be tracked and documented</li>
                <li>All members are expected to carry equal weight in the project workload</li>
            </ul>
        </div>
        
        <h2>Accountability Measures</h2>
        
        <div class="section-box">
            <h3>Documentation of Contributions</h3>
            <ul>
                <li>All team members must log their contributions in the project tracking system</li>
                <li>Weekly progress reports are required from each team member</li>
                <li>Peer evaluations will be conducted at project milestones</li>
            </ul>
        </div>
        
        <h3>Addressing Non-Contribution</h3>
        
        <div class="escalation-step">
            <strong>First Instance:</strong>
            <ul style="margin-top: 10px;">
                <li>Team lead or designated member will document the issue</li>
                <li>Private conversation with non-contributing member to understand barriers</li>
                <li>Written improvement plan with specific deliverables and timeline</li>
            </ul>
        </div>
        
        <div class="escalation-step">
            <strong>Second Instance:</strong>
            <ul style="margin-top: 10px;">
                <li>Formal documentation submitted to project advisor/instructor</li>
                <li>Mandatory meeting with team and advisor to address concerns</li>
                <li>Revised contribution agreement with stricter deadlines</li>
            </ul>
        </div>
        
        <div class="warning-box">
            <h2>No Acceptable Excuses Policy</h2>
            <p>While we recognize that life circumstances can be challenging, acceptance into this capstone project represents a commitment to contribute regardless of:</p>
            <ul>
                <li>Other coursework or academic obligations</li>
                <li>Work or employment responsibilities</li>
                <li>Personal scheduling preferences</li>
                <li>Technical difficulties or resource limitations</li>
                <li>Disagreements with team decisions or direction</li>
            </ul>
            <p style="margin-top: 15px;"><strong>Team members who anticipate significant barriers to contribution should:</strong></p>
            <ol style="margin-left: 40px;">
                <li>Communicate proactively with the team and advisor</li>
                <li>Develop a modified contribution plan that maintains equitable workload</li>
                <li>Seek available accommodations through team leader & project advisor</li>
            </ol>
        </div>
        
        <h2>Conflict Resolution</h2>
        <div class="section-box">
            <p>When disputes arise regarding contributions:</p>
            <ol style="margin-left: 40px;">
                <li>Team members should first attempt to resolve issues internally</li>
                <li>If unresolved, involve the team leader or project advisor</li>
                <li>Document all discussions and agreed-upon solutions</li>
                <li>Implement solutions with clear timelines and checkpoints</li>
            </ol>
        </div>
        
        <h2>Support and Resources</h2>
        <div class="section-box">
            <p>The team and institution commit to providing:</p>
            <ul>
                <li>Clear project requirements and expectations</li>
                <li>Access to necessary tools, software, and resources</li>
                <li>Regular advisor check-ins and guidance</li>
                <li>Reasonable accommodations for documented needs through official channels</li>
            </ul>
        </div>
        
        <h2>Recognition of Contribution</h2>
        <div class="section-box">
            <p>Teams that successfully complete the capstone with full participation from all members will:</p>
            <ul>
                <li>Receive individual contribution recognition in final documentation</li>
                <li>Have the opportunity to present their completed work</li>
                <li>Receive full academic credit for the capstone project</li>
            </ul>
        </div>
        
        <h2>Amendment Process</h2>
        <div class="section-box">
            <p>This Code of Conduct may only be amended with:</p>
            <ul>
                <li>Unanimous agreement from all team members</li>
                <li>Approval from the project advisor</li>
                <li>Written documentation of changes and rationale</li>
            </ul>
        </div>
        
        <h2>Acknowledgment</h2>
        <p>By participating in this capstone project, all team members acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agree to abide by this Code of Conduct. Each member commits to contributing fully to the project's success.</p>
